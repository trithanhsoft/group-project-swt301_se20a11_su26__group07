# Testing

Bo tai lieu nay duoc xay dung tu codebase hien tai va cac tai lieu trong `documents/`, tap trung vao feature thuc te dang co trong he thong thay vi chi bam scope V1 ban dau.

Quy mo hien tai:
- Hon 300 testcase/scenario, tach theo feature va luong regression xuyen module.

Nguon doi chieu chinh:
- `documents/AI_GUIDE.md`
- `documents/STRUCTURE.md`
- `documents/SET_UP.md`
- `documents/*_PLAN.md`
- `documents/Test_Plan.docx`
- `documents/Test_Features.docx`
- `documents/Test_Case_Specification.docx`
- `backend/src/modules/**`
- `frontend/src/features/**`

Luu y mapping giao dien hien tai:
- User Management nam trong tab `Quan ly tai khoan` cua `/admin/hr?tab=users`
- Recipe Management nam trong tab `Dinh luong cong thuc` cua `/admin/products?tab=recipes`
- Stock Forecast va Transaction History nam trong `/admin/stock`
- Attendance cua staff nam trong tab `Cham cong` tren `/staff/hr`, admin co trang `/admin/hr/attendance`
